Enabling the passionately curious

IBM Makes is a platform for creators throughout IBM to express themselves and the hobbies that enrich their lives, while also bringing that creative energy back into their careers.

With the breadth and depth of the outlets we have—from printmaking and circuitry to magazines and radio—we’re enabling every IBMer to dive into creative endeavors without judgment or criticism. People at IBM are curious, and we’re doing our part to allow that curiosity to blossom.
